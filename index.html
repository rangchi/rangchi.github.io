Work Experience

2015 - 2019

System Engineer / ISC, Tehran, CBI Datacenter

ISC is the leadership company in providing banking systems to almost all commercial banks of Iran as well as many financial institutions. Headquartered in Tehran and with multiple offices countrywide, ISC is the largest electronic banking organization in the Middle East Region. Central Bank of Iran is one of its main customers, which our department’s focus is mainly maintaining CBI datacenter in all aspects such as administering virtualized systems, maintaining SAN Storages and SAN switches, planning backup strategies and designing new subsystems based on current infrastructure.

My responsibilities as a system engineer in Operations department include but are not limited to:

  • Leading a team to accomplish CBI requests in all datacenter layers such as OS, Server, VM, SAN, Backup and so on.
  • Implement, Design and Administration of VMware based virtual datacenter.
  • Troubleshooting VMware vSphere 6/6.5 servers and systems.
  • Install and troubleshoot Linux (Redhat and CentOS) based OSes.
  • Maintaining Linux and Windows OSes providing critical banking services such as CORE Banking.
  • Configuring EMC VNX 5800 SAN Storages and tuning it for virtualized environment.
  • Planning and implementing backup restore strategies using VEEAM Backup 9.5 integrated with EMC VNX SAN storages, SCALAR i40 tape libraries and HP StoreOnce 4700.
  • Optimizing VEEAM backup with the help of direct SAN backup to minimize backup time.
  • Install, configure and manage VEEAM One 9.5.
  • Implementing disaster recovery site with the help of VMware SRM, EMC Mirrorview and VEEAM Backup and Replication.
  • Install and configure Redhat Satellite Server in a disconnected environment to update and patch Redhat Linux VMs.
  • Install and configure vRealize Operations Manager, vRealize Log Insight and VMware Update Manager.
  • Providing basic support for HP DL 380/580 G7/G8/G9/G10 servers such as configuring RAID controller, updating firmware through SPP and troubleshooting hardware errors based on iLO alerts.
  • Tuning Oracle database VMs in terms of storage IO bottlenecks with the help of EMC VNX FastChache, FastVP, VMware Multipath and Linux LVM.
  • Providing technical support for Safenet Luna SA 4/5 HSM devices with regard to the deployed NEXUS PKI infrastructure.
  • Responsible for the maintenance of HP, IBM and Brocade SAN Switches in terms of implementing Zoning, implementing full redundancy and troubleshooting hardware errors.
  • Administering F5 ASM (WAF) devices
